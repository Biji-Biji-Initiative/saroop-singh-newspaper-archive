SELANGOR ATHLETIC CHAMPIONSHIPS.
Sikh Runner's State Record For Half-Mile

(From Our Own Correspondent)
Kuala Lumpur, July 17.

SAROOP SINGH, the V.I.O.B.A. runner, was reported as the only competitor to break an existing State record. The scan gives his half-mile time as 2 min. 6 4/5 sec., lowering by one second the previous 2 min. 7 4/5 sec. mark associated with Fauja Singh.
